TERIYAKI BOX

A savory and aromatic Japanese-inspired blend. We wok-fire fresh garlic, ginger, onions, peppers, and Asian vegetables in a **rich, glossy teriyaki glaze**. This classic flavor is balanced with a distinct sweet-and-tangy profile for a deeply satisfying experience.

Recommended with **HOKKIEN** noodles.

SPICY PEANUT NOODLE BOX

A journey to Southeast Asia in a bowl. This **rich and creamy Indonesian-style peanut sauce** is built on a base of **coconut milk**, with a gentle kick of chili heat. It's wok-fired with fresh vegetables and topped with crunchy roasted peanuts and aromatic cilantro.

Recommended with **RIBBON** noodles.
