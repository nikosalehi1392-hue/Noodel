A JOURNEY GLOBAL

Noodles are the world's universal comfort food. Explore three iconic dishes that define culture, comfort, and cuisine across continents.

       
       
                       
               
                                   
JAPAN
               

TONKOTSU RAMEN

               

                    A masterpiece of Japanese cuisine. **Tonkotsu** features chewy, thin wheat noodles swimming in a rich, creamy, 18-hour-simmered **pork bone broth**. It's traditionally topped with succulent chashu pork, a soft-boiled egg, and wood ear mushrooms.                

               

                    **Key Flavors:** Umami, Savory, Creamy                

           
                       
               
                                   
THAILAND
               

PAD THAI

               

                    Thailand's national dish is a perfect balance of **sweet, sour, salty, and spicy**. Flat rice noodles are stir-fried with eggs, tofu, dried shrimp, tamarind paste, and garnished heavily with fresh cilantro, spring onions, and crushed peanuts.                

               

                    **Key Flavors:** Sweet, Sour, Nutty                

           
                       
               
                                   
ITALY
               

CACIO E PEPE

               

                    The ultimate Roman comfort food. Its genius lies in its simplicity, using just four ingredients: **pasta (usually spaghetti), Pecorino Romano cheese, black pepper**, and the starchy water used to cook the noodles, forming a rich emulsion.                

               

                    **Key Flavors:** Pungent, Peppery, Salty
