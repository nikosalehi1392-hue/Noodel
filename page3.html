SEE THE 4 STEPS

Our Signature Method

1

PREPARE FRESH INGREDIENTS

We start by finely slicing high-quality vegetables and preparing our protein bases. Freshness is paramount to the final taste and texture of every dish.

2

THE WOK HEI FLASH-FRY

Noodles are flash-fried in an authentic wok over high heat with a touch of oil to achieve the sought-after 'Wok Hei' smoky flavor and texture.

3

SAUCE INFUSION

Our signature sauces (Teriyaki, Peanut, Kung Pao) are added quickly, coating the noodles and vegetables perfectly under intense heat.

4

GARNISH & SEAL

Each box is finished with recommended garnishes like fresh sprouts, roasted peanuts, or cilantro before being sealed for ultimate freshness.
